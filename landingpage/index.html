Plot Bunni

An open-source novel writing tool to organize ideas, structure stories, and leverage AI for brainstorming and drafting. Access it directly in your browser, works seamlessly on your desktop and mobile devices!

Launch App Fork on GitHub

Features

Comprehensive Novel Overview & Planning

Visualize your novel's structure at a glance. Manage acts, chapters, and scenes with an intuitive planning interface. Link story elements and keep your plot organized.

  • Centralized novel management grid.
  • Hierarchical story planning (acts, chapters, scenes).
  • Detailed novel information: synopsis, author, cover.
  • Import story outlines from text.

Rich Concept Management & Focused Writing

Organize all your story's building blocks—characters, locations, lore, and more—in one place. Then, immerse yourself in a distraction-free writing space designed for focus, with helpful tools like Markdown support and easy navigation.

  • Easily manage all your story elements, such as characters, locations, and lore.
  • Write your story scene by scene using a flexible editor with Markdown support for formatting.
  • Enjoy a seamless writing flow with text areas that grow with your content and built-in formatting assistance.
  • Jump to any part of your novel quickly using an intuitive outline navigator.

Deep Customization & Local Data

Tailor Plotbunni to your liking with extensive theme options, including light/dark modes, presets, and individual color customization. All your data stays secure and accessible offline, stored locally in your browser.

  • Light, Dark, and System theme modes.
  • Predefined theme presets (Sepia, Paper, etc.).
  • Full control over individual theme colors.
  • Local data storage via IndexedDB for offline access.
  • Export novel projects (JSON) and content (Markdown, Text, PDF).

Intelligent AI Assistance

Break through writer's block with AI-powered suggestions for scene synopses, outlines, descriptions, and full scene text. Configure AI models and prompts to fit your needs.

  • Contextual AI text suggestions.
  • AI Novel Writer for drafting entire sections.*
  • Customizable AI endpoint profiles and prompts.
  • Live streaming of AI suggestions.

*AI Novel Writer is experimental and best for rapid prototyping.
